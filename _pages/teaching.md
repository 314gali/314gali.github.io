# Cryptography, M1, 2019 - 2023, Université Versailles Saint-Quentin

- For Louis Goubin - 120h - 10 courses of 3h  for 4 years
- Topics: Cryptanalysis of histroric block-ciphers among which Enigma, Cryptanalysis of DES et AES, modes, time-memory tradeoff, the birthday paradox, message authentication codes (MAC), the RSA primitive and an introduction to fault attacks

# Secure Computation, M1, 2021 - 2023, Université Versailles Saint-Quentin

- For Louis Goubin - 48h - 8 courses of 3h for 2 years.
- Topics: Simple Power Analysis, Fault attacks, Buffer Overflow attacks, Multiparty Computation and Homomorhpic encryption.

# Introduction to Cryptography, L3, 2020 - 2021, Université Versailles Saint-Quentin

- For Christina Boura - 36h de TD - 12 courses of 3h
- Topics: Enigma, Stream Ciphers (based on
LFSR) et Block Ciphers (based on Feistels like DES), modes, RSA, ElGamal and the Diffie-Hellman protocol.

# Mathematics for Computer Science, L2, 2019 - 2020, Université Versailles Saint-Quentin

- Christina Boura - 36h - 12 courses of 3h 
- Topics : Proof by induction, logic for mathematical proofs, functions, equivalence relations, groups, rings and fields, GCD, Euclid's algorithm and basic linear algebra.
